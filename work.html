Homing Pigeon - Animated Shortfilm

Born on a random weekend in September of 2023, my brother and I challenged ourselves to create an entirely animated shortfilm as quickly as we could. Homing Pigeon is a simple story about a homing pigeon delivering a love letter from a boyfriend in the city to his long distance girlfriend in the suburbs across the world. We decided to use both of our strenghts into creating this project, with my brother being in charge of the three-dimensional backgrounds, general compsiting, and assembly, and me being in charge of character design, two-dimensional character animation, and original score. Today, Homing Pigeon is the proud owner of three film festival awards: Grand Canyon University's Best Alumni Film, and the UNLV Spring Flicks's 3rd Place Audience Vote and The Best Short Short.
